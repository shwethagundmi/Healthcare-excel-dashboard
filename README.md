# Title:
"Chronic Disease Risk Assessment Dashboard"
# Dashboard Preview:
[Dashboard] (Healthcare Dashboard.png)
# Project Overview:
Analyzed a dataset of 2000 patients to understand how lifestyle factors like smoking, diet, BMI, and physical activity affect diseases such as heart disease and diabetes using an Excel dashboard.
# Tools used:
Microsoft Excel, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers.
# Dashboard Charts Explanation:
1. **Pie Chart:** Average weight of patients with different cholestrol levels who perform physical activity.
2. **Stacked Bar Chart:** Risk level of patients based pn smoking habit.
3. **Column Chart:** Varioud patients and their BMI levels.
4. **Pie Chart:** Medical history and Family history of patients.
# Key Insights:
1. Smoking increases the risk of chronic diseases.
2. Non-Vegetarian is most common diet type among all patients.
3. Heart disease and Diabetes patients are more commom among females compared to males.
4. Average BMI for both famae ad male is greater than 25 which indicates Overweight.
# Conclusion:
The analysis of 2000 patients reveals that chronic diseases are linked to lifestyle factors. The data highlights a strong relationship between lifestyle choices such as physical activity, diet type, smoking habit and chronic diseases (heart disease, diabetes).
Improving physical activity, reducing smoking and managing cholesterol levels significantly reduce risk levels.
